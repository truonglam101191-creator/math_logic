info How to Play

Welcome to Tidy Tinker! Your goal is to organize all 10 items by dragging each one to its correct slot in the compartment tray.

Playing:
• Drag items with your finger or mouse
• Each item has a uniquely shaped slot (rectangles, circles, keyholes, wedges, and more!)
• Items will gently rotate if untouched for 15+ seconds
• When you find the right spot, it snaps with sparkles and a satisfying sound!
• Use the hint button to highlight the next item to place
• Complete all items for a magical celebration!

Creating (Edit Mode):
• Use the Creator Tools panel for comprehensive editing
• Visual dropdowns show shape previews and difficulty indicators
• Real-time property editing: colors, opacity, rotation, size
• Scaling Controls: Drag corner/edge handles to resize objects
• Grid system with snapping for precise alignment
• Layer depth controls and alignment tools
• "Test Play" to preview your level as players will experience it
• "Preview" mode hides editing guides for clean view
• Auto-save keeps your level changes safe (edit mode only)
• Level Switching: Your edits are automatically saved when switching levels!
• Recovery: Use the "Recover" button if you ever lose edits
• Keyboard Shortcuts:
  • 'G' to toggle grid, 'Delete' to remove selected objects
  • '[' and ']' to rotate objects
  • '+' and '-' to scale objects uniformly
  • Arrow keys to move (Ctrl+Arrow to resize width/height)
  • Shift+Arrow for faster movement/resizing
